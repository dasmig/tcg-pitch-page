Generation Pipeline

From Prompt to Playable in 4 Stages

One sentence from a user becomes a fully illustrated, mechanically balanced, playable card series.

1

Series Bible

The LLM expands a user prompt like "Radioactive Insects" into a thematic universe — lore, aesthetic tone, and mechanical identity (e.g., Scaling Yield + Sabotage).

2

Card Generation

Each card receives a rarity-based point budget. The LLM maps natural language to engine mechanics via structured JSON. +10 Chips = 1pt, x1.5 Mult = 8pt, Retrigger = 12pt.

3

Art Synthesis

A seed image establishes the series' visual identity. IP-Adapter enforces style consistency. ControlNet guides composition. Layered compositing renders final card frames.

4

Balance Validation

Headless RL agents simulate thousands of matches. If win-rate deviation exceeds 55%, the pipeline auto-nerfs stats and re-runs until the balance fitness function converges.

Core Innovation

Accumulation Scoring, Not Attrition

Cards are mathematical operators — not combat units. They generate chips, add multipliers, and apply exponential scaling. Order of operations is the primary skill vector.

♦

Chips (Base Value)

Additive foundation. High-volume, low-impact. The AI can generate +100 to +300 without breaking balance because chips scale poorly alone.

✦

+Mult (Additive Multiplier)

First layer of polynomial growth. Ranges +4 to +30. Bridging early to mid-game. Without it, chips are meaningless.

×

xMult (Exponential)

The engine of runaway scaling. Restricted to x1.5 – x4 and bound to conditional triggers. A single x3 outscales +30 in late game.

Competitive Design

3-Lane PvP with Simultaneous Turns

Inspired by Marvel Snap and Gwent. Players split resources across 3 scoring lanes. Win 2 of 3 to take the round. Simultaneous reveal eliminates first-mover advantage. Priority goes to the losing player — a built-in catch-up mechanic.

🎯

Lane Dominance

Each lane resolves its own Chips × Mult × xMult independently. Commit your best cards to one lane, or spread thin to contest all three?

🔀

Sabotage Mechanics

Sequence Scramble randomizes opponent card order. Semantic Silence disables tagged abilities. Degradation permanently nerfs scaling cards.

⚡

Nemesis Rounds

Extended matches use life tokens. Lose a round → lose a life. Tests deck consistency over multiple antes, not just a lucky draw.
